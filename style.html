Deolali Pravara is a town located in the Rahuri Taluka of Ahmednagar District, Maharashtra, India.

In Deolali Pravara, 12% of the population is under 6 years of age.

It has a population of 30,334 as of the 2001 census and is governed by the Deolali Pravara Municipal Council.

Key features of Deolali Pravara: Location: Rahuri Taluka, Ahmednagar District, Maharashtra. Type: Class I Cantonment, established in 1869. Population: 30,997 (2011 census). Elevation: 2,000 feet. Surroundings: Sahyadri mountain range. Known for: Hill station and health resort. Pin Code: 413716. Education: Notable educational institutes include Barnes School, Army Public School, and St Patrick's Convent High School. Municipal Council: Deolali Pravara Municipal Council, a "C" class council with 18 wards.
